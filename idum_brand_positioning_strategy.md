# Positioning

## Write Your Obituary

**International Dialogues on Underwater Munitions (IDUM)**\
*2010s--2040s*

IDUM was the organization that revealed one of the greatest hidden
environmental crises in human history --- the millions of tons of
military munitions dumped into the world's oceans after the two world
wars.

What began as a small international dialogue led by explosive ordnance
expert Terrence P. Long became the global catalyst that transformed how
nations understood and managed underwater munitions.

Through relentless research, international convenings, scientific
partnerships, and policy advocacy, IDUM united governments, scientists,
and institutions that had previously treated the issue in isolation.

Their work helped establish the first international treaty framework for
underwater munitions management, created global monitoring standards,
and mapped thousands of previously undocumented dump sites.

Most importantly, IDUM shifted the global mindset: from denial and
fragmentation to responsibility and cooperation.

Future generations inherited cleaner oceans because IDUM made the
invisible visible.
